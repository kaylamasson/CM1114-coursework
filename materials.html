Materials

Hover over each image to find out more!

Crochet hooks are available in multiple different sizes, usually between 2mm and 10mm. The hook you use will depend on the weight of your yarn and the tightness of your stitches.

The yarn you choose depends on the project you want to create and the desired outcome. For beginners we would recommend yarn which requires a 4mm hook.

Scissors are useful for finishing off your project and cutting your crochet from the ball of yarn.

A darning needle is like a sewing needle but has an eye large enough to thread yarn. This is handy for sewing crochet parts together.

If you plan on making toys or 3D projects then you will need some toy stuffing.

Stitch markers help you to keep count of the amount of rows or stitches you have done. Sometimes when crocheting something round it can be easy to lose track of the start and end point of the row.
