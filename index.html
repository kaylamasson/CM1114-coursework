Welcome

Welcome to the RGU's Crochet Society! Whether you are looking to pick up a new skill or need a way to relax and wind down, our crochet society is perfect for you!

We meet up regularly to crochet and chat, as well as having fun nights out to get to know each other!

Whether you are an experienced crocheter or a complete beginner you are welcome to join us!
