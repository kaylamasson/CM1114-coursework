Welcome!

Welcome to the RGU's Crochet Society! Whether you are looking to pick up a new skill or you are wanting a way to relax and wind down, our crochet society is perfect for you!

We meet up regularly to crochet and chat, as well as having fun nights out to get to know each other!

Everyone, from experienced crocheters to complete beginners, is welcome so come and join us!
